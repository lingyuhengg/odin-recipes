Curry

This is a recipe for Chicken Curry made using an Instant Pot. For a more precise recipe, click here.

Ingredients

  • 450g Chicken, cut to bite-size chunks
  • 3 Tablespoons glee/oil
  • 5g Ginger, finely chopped
  • 5 Cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 Tablespoon lemon juice
  • 5g Cilantro

Spices

  • 1 Black cardamom
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 6 Cloves
  • 6 Back peppercorns
  • 0.5 Teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 Teaspoons coriander powder
  • 0.5 Teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 0.5 Teaspoon Garam masala
  • 0.25 Teaspoon Ground turmeric
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt

Instructions

  1. Fry Aromatics

    Set the Instant Pot to "Saute" and add oil into the pot. Add the whole spices (non-powders) and fry unti fragrant. Once fragrant, add the gree chili, onions, ginger and garlic and keep stirring until they start to turn golden brown.

  2. Fry Remaining Ingredients

    Add the chopped tomatoes and powdered spices, and saute for 4 minutes. After that, add the chicken and saute for another 4 minutes. For some extra spice, chili peppers such as Thai/Birdeye Chilis are recommended.

  3. Add Stock/Water

    Add about 125g of any stock of choice or water, and stir the pot to mix up everything as well as deglaze the pot. For an even thicker texture, add some coconut cream or heavy cream.

  4. Pressure Cook the Curry

    Seal the pot and set it to pressure cook on high for 5 minutes. Once done, quick release the pressure and mix in the lemon juice.

  5. Serve

    Garnish the curry with cilantro and it can now be served, along with a side of rice.
